東京スカイツリー
(Tokyo Skytree)

Homepage Info

The tokyo skytree is a neofuturistic broadcasting tower reaching 634 meters in height. The reason for this height was from the old country name of musashi no kuni, in which musashi can be turned into numbers corresponding to 634 in the japanese language. Though at the start of construction it was planned ata 610.8m height the news of the GuangZhou TV tower being 610 m in height made them to go for the record height of a singular broadcasting tower of 634m. This building starts as a triangle and gradually as height goes up, becomes a circle. In a country constantly in the threat of an earthquake this choice was a must to ensure the building will not fall. It also has a restaurant, aquarium, and a gift shop near the base or sometimes near the observation decks.
