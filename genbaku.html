原爆ドーム
(Hiroshima Peace Memorial)

Homepage Info

The Hiroshima Peace Memorial was once the Hiroshima prefectural property display house and shows the items made in Hiroshima. Built in 1915 it was a five story building with a dome on the top of the fifth floor. But this building wouldn’t keep its shape for long, In 1945 the atom bomb went off very close to the building wiping away any traces of the building around them and instantly killing the people with it. Now only the frame of the dome and some of the floors walls remain. Spending around 1,000,000 yen or $9,000 on finding a way to preserve the building it became a world heritage site, representing the dangers of nuclear warfare and the 2 hundreds of thousands live lost during the bombing.
